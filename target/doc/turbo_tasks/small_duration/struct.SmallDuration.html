pub struct SmallDuration<const P: u64>(_);
Expand description

Stores a Duration in a given precision (in nanoseconds) in 4 bytes.

For instance, for P = 10_000 (10 microseconds), this allows a for a total duration of 11.9 hours. Values smaller than 10 microseconds are stored as 10 microseconds.
